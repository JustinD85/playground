* Number Sets
** Legend
*** R : Real Numbers
*** R-Q : Irrational Numbers
*** Q : Rational Numbers
*** Z : Integers
*** W : Whole Numbers
*** N : Natural Numbers

** Definition
*** R : Any Number
*** R-Q: R That Is Not Q 
*** Q : Can be written as a fraction or decimal
*** Z : W and their complements
*** W : N and 0 
*** N : {1,2,3,...99,100,...n}

* Identity Numbers
** Definition
*** Identity : Apply X to Y and the result is Y
*** Examples
**** 0 + 1 = 1
**** 1 * 4 = 4

* Opposite(additive inverse)
** Definition
*** X and Y where they are equidistant from 0
